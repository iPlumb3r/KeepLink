Change Level
==
Levels in KL
-
There is 3 "Standard" level in KL :
* Normal
* Advanced
* Designer

Note : Others level (e.g "Packager" can be unlock thanks to specific "Easter Egg")

How to change level in KL
-
* Swap the bottom bar to rigth
* Open "Settings" menu
* Select "KeepLink level" Property
* Choose you level
